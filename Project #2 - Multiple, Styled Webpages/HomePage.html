Overview

Otters are carnivorous mammals in the subfamily Lutrinae. The 13 extant otter species are all semiaquatic,
aquatic, or marine, with diets based on fish and invertebrates. Lutrinae is a branch of the Mustelidae family,
which also includes weasels, badgers, mink, and wolverines, among other animals.
